🏔️ River Run

Whitewater kayak — navigate rapids, eddies & stoppers

Quick start: Press W to paddle forward, A / D to steer. The river current carries you — dodge rocks!

🛶 How to Play

You're kayaking down a whitewater river! The current carries you downstream automatically — your job is to steer around rocks, navigate rapids, and survive as far as possible.

🎯 Goal

Travel as far downstream as you can. Avoid crashing into rocks and canyon walls. Use eddies (calm water behind rocks) to rest. Watch out for stoppers (hydraulics) that can trap you!

📱 Mobile / Touch

Tap the left circle to paddle left, right circle to paddle right. Use the toolbar buttons for pause, mute, and fullscreen.

🌊 River Features

Rapids ⚠ — Steeper sections with big waves and fast current. A warning flashes on screen. Paddle hard and steer carefully!
Eddies — Calm circular water behind large rocks. Paddle into them to slow down and rest.
Stoppers — Recirculating water at steep drops. They pull you back! Paddle forward hard (W) to escape.
Rocks — Grey boulders in the river. Collisions slow you down and flash the screen red — steer around them!

🕹️ Game Modes

Arcade — Intuitive controls. Press A to turn left, D to turn right.
Simulation — Realistic. Left paddle stroke turns you right (like real kayaking).
